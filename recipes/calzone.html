calzone

Description

Delicious folded pizza dough filled with cheese,meats,vegetables, and baked until golden perfection.

Ingredients

  • Mozerella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Mushrooms
  • Bell peppers
  • Marinara saucee

Steps

  1. Roll out pizza dough
  2. Add desired fillings
  3. Fold dough over fillings,seals edges
  4. Bake until golden brown and enjoy
